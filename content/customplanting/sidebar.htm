Measuring Tips

  1. With a tape measure, locate the boundaries of your site and sketch them on paper to create a base map.
  2. Measure structures and plants you wish to keep, utility boxes, hose spigots, and windows, noting their sill height from the ground.
  3. Write all measurements and an arrowing pointing north on your base map.
  4. Mark areas that are particularly soggy, dry, sunny, shady, or have poor soil conditions.
  5. Indicate views to keep and those to block
