Wreathmaking Classes 2021

Wreathmaking Classes Are Back!

Portland Nursery is happy to announce the return of our in-person wreathmaking classes at both our SE Stark and SE Division Street shops! There are two types of classes available this year, and the dates, details, and registration requirements can be found below.

Private Group Parties:

Friends, families, and coworkers can sign up now for Private Group Parties at both of our SE Portland shops!

5050 SE Stark • 9000 SE Division

Nov 19 through Dec 19, 2021

10am • 12:30pm • 3pm

Class Duration: All Private Group Parties are scheduled for 1.5 hours

Party Size: Minimum of 5 people and maximum of 10

Price: $39.99 per person

Includes: 5lbs of greens, wire, wreath ring, and a bow (cut floral is not included, but may be purchased on site during the party)

Other Info: Space heaters will be on site, but it’ll be getting a little chilly, so please dress warmly. We do encourage everyone to bring gardening gloves and pruners from home, but sanitized pruners will be available for use for those who need them. Masks are required.

To Register: All you need to do is call the location you’d like to reserve a time and date for and we’ll handle the rest! Payment will be due on the day of the Group Party.

Open Workshops:

If your party doesn’t meet the minimum size requirement for a Private Group Party, participating in one of our Wreathmaking Workshops at Stark or Division is the way to go!
