Get a Portland Nursery designed planting plan to help take the guesswork out of plant selection.

We use measurements and photos that you provide to create a custom plan after consulting with you on practical and aesthetic direction.

Plans are available for ready-to-plant yards that are not overly sloped and already have the hardscape installed. We design plant combinations only, no hardscape design or overly sloped spaces.

Turnaround depends on the season, typically 1-3 weeks from consultation appointment to finished plan.

Once you have a plan, we can further help with pulling plants together for pick-up or local delivery.

Please contact us at customplan@portlandnursery.com if you have questions.
