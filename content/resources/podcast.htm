A Podcast for New Gardeners

John and Katie are back with Season 2 of Growing Green Thumbs with Portland Nursery, and they’ll be covering a wide range of topics on everything from winter interest plantings to herbs and edible flowers. You’ll also be hearing from a number of new guests as they dive deep into their areas of interest and expertise, while also sharing stories about how their passion for plants has grown throughout their lives.

New episodes air every Friday on all of your favorite podcasting platforms.

About the Show

People aren’t born with green thumbs, they have to grow them, and Portland Nursery is here to help show you just how easy it is. Join John and Katie as they explore all things plants from the perspective of both a novice and experienced gardener in Growing Green Thumbs with Portland Nursery.
